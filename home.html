What is Gold-B's Agent?

Gold-B's Agent is a locally-installed automation platform that connects your WhatsApp Business account to an AI assistant. It processes incoming messages, matches them against configurable keyword rules, and delivers templated or AI-generated responses — all running on your own machine.

The platform runs as two Docker containers on your computer: a gateway that handles WhatsApp messaging, and an admin panel for configuration. All data stays on your hardware — nothing is stored on external servers controlled by us.

Key Features

AI-Powered Responses

Supports multiple AI providers (Gemini, OpenAI, Claude, Grok, Groq, Qwen) for intelligent, context-aware replies.

Rule-Based Automation

Keyword matching with template responses, lead routing, and multi-channel forwarding.

Knowledge Base (RAG)

Upload documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT) and let the AI retrieve relevant information to answer questions.

Google Integrations

Connect Google Sheets, Drive, Gmail, and Calendar for automated data logging and notifications.

Google API Integrations

Gold-B's Agent integrates with the following Google services to enhance automation workflows. Each integration is optional and can be enabled or disabled independently by the user.

Google Sheets — Lead logging & data export Google Drive — Document storage Gmail — Email reading & sending Google Calendar — Event reminders

How It Works

1. Install — Run the installer on your Windows or Linux machine. It sets up Docker containers automatically.

2. Configure — Open the admin panel at localhost:18790 to set up keywords, rules, templates, and integrations.

3. Connect — Scan the WhatsApp QR code and optionally connect your Google account via OAuth 2.0.

4. Automate — The agent processes incoming messages 24/7, routing them through your configured rules and AI providers.
